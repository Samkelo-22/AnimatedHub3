About AnimatedHub

A start-up dedicated to showcasing original animations and teaching the craft through a community-based learning center.

Our Mission

To become the default platform that animators and fans produce and refer to, with a selection of original animations and a comprehensive learning hub.

Our Vision

To democratise the learning process of animation and give a global stage to digital storytellers, regardless of their background or resources.

Our Values

Creativity, Collaboration, Accessibility, and Innovation drive everything we do at AnimatedHub.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, AnimatedHub emerged from a simple observation: while animation tools were becoming more accessible, quality learning resources and community support remained fragmented. Our founders, a team of animators and educators, set out to create a unified platform where aspiring and professional animators could learn, create, and connect.
