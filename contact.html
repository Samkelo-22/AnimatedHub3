Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to review animation submissions?

We typically review submissions within 2-3 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your animation has been reviewed.

Do you offer animation courses for complete beginners?

Yes! Our learning section includes beginner-friendly tutorials that assume no prior animation experience. We start with the fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced techniques.

Can I collaborate with other animators on the platform?

Absolutely! Our community features include collaboration tools, project sharing, and team formation capabilities to help you work with other animators.

Is there a cost to join AnimatedHub?

AnimatedHub offers both free and premium membership options. The free tier includes access to basic tutorials and community features, while premium unlocks advanced courses and exclusive content.
